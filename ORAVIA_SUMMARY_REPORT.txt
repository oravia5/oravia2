2. SMART & STRICT NSFW BUSINESS LOGIC
-------------------------------------------------------------------
- Logged-in Users (isAuthenticated):
  * Zero Blur / Zero Warning Overlay: Logged-in users NEVER see warning popups or blur overlays anywhere on Oravia.
  * Toggle OFF (showNSFW = false): Backend query filters out NSFW posts/videos/snips from public feeds (Timeline, Search, Hashtags, Locations, Snips). On profiles, saved, archive, and direct post links, posts render crystal clear without any warning popup.
  * Toggle ON (showNSFW = true): All NSFW content renders crystal clear everywhere.
- Unregistered Guest Visitors (!isAuthenticated):
  * Shared profiles and post links load without login redirects.
  * Any post flagged isNSFW: true displays with an 18+ Age Content Warning Blur Overlay ("Tap to view 18+") to protect minors.
